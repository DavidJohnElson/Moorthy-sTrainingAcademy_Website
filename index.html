ABOUT MOORTHY'S TRAINING ACADEMY

“Success is a choice, so choose it.”


We are a new generation privately owned Training and Recruitment Solutions provider. We have been primarily focusing on Company Specific Training Modules and Placement Opportunities for studious Candidates.
Founded in the mid - 2018’s by a Passionate Placement Coach MR. VigneshMK with strong expertise in Training and Development Services.Over the three years, the company has continued to strengthen its business across South India.

FOUNDER

VIGNESH MK

Vignesh, a budding post graduate professional with a decade of experience in Training, Recruitment , ITES & Customer Service. He started his career as a Skill Development Trainer with Kerala State Government Project , Additional Skills Acquisition Program. He was also Promoted to the role of Training Manager in a span of one year , based on performance level achievements. He had brought his Training passion to different IIM Alumni enterprises across South India. He has trained more than 1 lakh students with a consistent outstanding feedback.

CEO

POOJA MORKAR VIGNESH
